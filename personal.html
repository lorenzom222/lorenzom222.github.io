    To craft a Redstone Torch in Minecraft, you'll need 1 stick and 1 piece of Redstone Dust. Simply place the stick in the center slot of the crafting grid and the Redstone Dust directly below it. This simple yet essential tool is invaluable for powering Redstone circuits and creating various contraptions in your Minecraft world.
